The Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster is widely regarded as the best drink in
existence. Its effect has been described as like having your brains
smashed out by a slice of lemon wrapped round a large gold brick.

The Guide lists the recipe as follows: take the juice from one bottle of
that Ol' Janx Spirit, pour it into a measure of water from the seas of
Santraginus V, allow three cubes of Arcturan Mega-gin to melt into it,
add four litres of Fallian marsh gas, over the back of a silver spoon
float a measure of Qualactin Hypermint extract, drop in the tooth of an
Algolian Suntiger, allow to fizzle, add the juice of one whole
Zamphuor, and finally, decorate with an olive.

Bar staff across the galaxy are advised that the drink is somewhat
expensive to make properly, on account of the difficulty involved in
obtaining fresh Zamphuor.

Recommended consumption: no more than one, ever, unless you happen to be
a large mauve creature with more than four arms.
