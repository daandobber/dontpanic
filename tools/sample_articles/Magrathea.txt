Magrathea is a legendary planet, believed by many to be a myth, whose
entire economy was based on the manufacture of custom-made luxury
planets for the very rich. Business, according to the more paranoid
historians, was so good that the whole of the rest of the galactic
economy eventually collapsed, since nobody could afford to buy their
other, less impressive, imported products any more.

Magrathea then vanished, its location wiped from every star chart,
possibly out of embarrassment, possibly out of very sound commercial
planning. It is said its inhabitants slept for aeons, waiting for the
economy to pick back up, keeping their planet-building factories on
standby just in case.

Visitors are, on the whole, not encouraged, on account of the automated
defence systems, which tend to open with polite recorded messages before
opening fire.
